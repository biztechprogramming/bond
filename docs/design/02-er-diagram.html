Database Distribution

  • SpacetimeDB (Live): High-frequency coordination. Handles Agents, Messages, Sessions, and Tasks. This is the "active memory" that clients subscribe to.
  • SQLite (knowledge.db): Semantic and relational history. Handles Emails, Content Chunks (Vectors), Entities, Memories, and Audit Logs. This is the "long-term memory" used for RAG and audits.
  • FileSystem (Blobs): Bulk storage. Content-addressed Blobs (attachments, screenshots) and raw Logs.
  • JSON/Encrypted (Config): Bootstrapping and secrets. bond.json for topology/settings and credentials.enc for encrypted tokens.
